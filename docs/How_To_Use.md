# How to Use This Website

## Pre-requisite

An email address with `sas.edu.sg` domain. This includes any teacher's or student's email address.

## Quick Start

1. Visit [www.sas-pathfinder.org](https://www.sas-pathfinder.org) in your browser

2. Click on the `login` button at the top right corner

3. Sign in with your school email address

4. Finish the onboarding page that you are redirected to

5. Once you are done, click `Start Exploring` and browse the site

> Do note it may take several seconds to load if it's your first time logging in

_If anything went wrong, or the site is not loading, kindly refresh the website or [report an issue](https://github.com/YianXie/path-finder/issues)_

## Advanced Techniques

### Add a Review

When viewing an item, click on the `LEAVE A REVIEW` button in the detail page to rate it. You can also write a comment and even upload a picture.

> **Note**: This only works for logged in user

### Save an Item

You can save an item by clicking on the heart button in the home page or in its detail page. After saving, you can view it in the `Saved` tab under your profile menu.

> **Note**: This only works for logged in user

### Compare Two Items

In the home page or your `Saved` page, hover over an item with your mouse and click on the checkbox at the top right corner to select it. After selecting two items, click on the `compare` button at the bottom right corner to compare them.
